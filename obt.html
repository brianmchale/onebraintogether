HOW TO MINT

  1. Click "Connect Wallet"
  2. Connect your Ethereum wallet (MetaMask or WalletConnect)
  3. Select the number of tokens you would like to mint - you can mint up to 10 tokens per wallet for FREE.
  4. Click "Mint Presale"
  5. Pay gas fee (requires ETH)
  6. Wait for confirmation - this should take 1-2 minutes.

If minting was successful, you will see a message that says "Successfully Minted Tokens". Once your tokens are minted, you can view and trade them by connecting your wallet to a secondary marketplace such as OpenSea. There will be a placeholder CD-ROM image until the reveal on Thursday, September 15th.

If you are having trouble minting, tell us about it on our Discord.

ABOUT

Inspired by the early internet, redesigned for the future internet.
Algorithmically generated from more than 150 traits celebrating DIY communities both IRL and online, Obt. is designed to be an art- and fashion-focused avatar project combining contemporary and nostalgic influences. Each token is unique and has a varying rarity level based on a combination of 4-8 attributes. Certain traits will unlock token-gated utility as we build the One Brain Together ecosystem.
