Middleground is a series of nine photography editions by Brian McHale. It is a companion collection to Obt., our digital collectible project. To mint, you will need an Ethereum wallet.

HOW TO MINT

  1. Click "Connect Wallet"
  2. Connect your Ethereum wallet
  3. Select the number of tokens you would like to mint
  4. Click "Mint Presale"
  5. Pay gas fee
  6. Wait for confirmation - this should take 1-2 minutes

If minting was successful, you will see a message that says Successfully Minted Tokens. Once your tokens are minted, you can view and trade them by connecting your wallet to a secondary marketplace such as OpenSea.

If you are having trouble minting, tell us about it on our Discord.

ABOUT MIDDLEGROUND

Middleground is a of nine 35mm photographs by Brian McHale. All photos were used as backgrounds in Obt., a digital collectible project by One Brain Together. There are 11 editions of each photo, with a randomized mint.

Approximately half of the collection will be available for Obt. collectors to claim for free during a limited presale window; one per wallet address. The rest of the collection will be available to the public following this period.

All collectors will receive the same community benefits as our Obt. token holders, with additional roles and utility available for collectors of both Obt. and Middleground. Join our Guild to gain access to token-gated channels and additional roles in our Discord server.
