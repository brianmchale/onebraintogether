NEW NEURONS is a collection by Brian McHale that explore the intersection of photography and AI art. Each image is run through a Generative Adversarial Network (GAN), where it is recreated by an AI either as a static image or a dynamic animation. Phase 1, available now on Foundation, consists of 10 Static Neurons and 1 Dynamic Neuron.
