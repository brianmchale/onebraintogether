NEW NEURONS AND A ROADMAP FOR ONE BRAIN TOGETHER

I’m iffy on roadmaps as they have been defined in this space, as a lot of the time they amount to nothing more than empty promises to serve as incentive to buy scam projects. That’s what we want to avoid here at all costs. But I want to share some more info on what I’ve been working on, and how it will impact what we do going forward.

NEW NEURONS will be the first point of entry into One Brain Together. 111 pieces that explore the intersection of photography and AI art — 100 Static Neurons, 10 Dynamic Neurons, and 1 Burnt Neuron.

NEW NEURONS act as your One Brain Together access token. Holders will receive the following utility:

  • Free admission to all One Brain Together events, the first of which will be in June 2022 during NFTNYC.
  • Creative contribution to community-owned projects
  • Exclusive airdrops & allowlist access
  • Lots more in the future as this project develops

Drop 1 will consist of 10 Static Neurons @ 0.1Ξ and 1 Dynamic Neuron to be given away to a collector.
Drop 2 will consist of 20 Static Neurons @ 0.1Ξ and 2 Dynamic Neurons - one to be given away to a collector, and one to be given away randomly.
Drop 3 will consist of 30 Static Neurons @ 0.1Ξ and 3 Dynamic Neurons to be given away.
Drop 4 will consist of 40 Static Neurons @ 0.1Ξ, 4 Dynamic Neurons to be given away, and 1 Burnt Neuron @ 1Ξ.
Each drop will have giveaways, as well as airdrops to holders.

If some of this terminology doesn't make sense to you, don't worry. We will make everything clear to all members as we go along.

As I anticipate this will not sell out instantly, we will be building in the mean time. In addition to releases and events from One Brain Together, we will be working to produce collaborative projects that will be collectively owned by our community. The first will be a digital zine that can be burned to redeem a physical copy.

Funds generated from the NEW NEURONS collection will be used to pay for the production of One Brain Together’s first full-length documentary and corresponding screening event(s). From there, we will update our community with the next phase of how we plan to venture further into the realms of film, photo, music, fashion, and product design as a community and build together across all mediums.

-Brian
