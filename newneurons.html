Roadmaps are often nothing more than empty promises that serve as incentive to buy into scams. That’s what we're not going to do.

NEW NEURONS is a collection of 111 pieces by Brian McHale that explore the intersection of photography and AI art. Each image is run through a Generative Adversarial Network (GAN), where it is recreated by an AI either as a Static image or a Dynamic animation. 100 Static Neurons and 10 Dynamic Neurons. NEW NEURONS is also the first point of entry into One Brain Together and acts as your community access token. Collectors will receive the following utility:

  • Free admission to all One Brain Together events
  • Creative contribution to community-owned projects
  • Exclusive airdrops, giveaways, & allowlist access
  • Apparel and more...

There will be four Phases to this project. Phase 1 begins March 22nd, 2022 on Foundation. It will consist of 10 Static Neurons and 1 Dynamic Neuron, all @ 0.1Ξ each.
